Planet Reproduction

Parts of a Flower

One flower has both the female and male reproductive systems. The stigma, style, ovary, ad ovule make up the female part ad the male part consists of the other and the filament.

Pollenation

Pollination is the transfer of pollen from the anther to a stigma to allow fertilization. Pollen from a flower’s anthers rubs or drops onto a pollinator, like a bee. The pollinator then take this pollen to another flower, where the pollen sticks to the stigma. When the polls lands on the stigma it creates a pollen tube to the ovary. The tip of the tube melts and the pollen nucleus fuses with the ovule's nucleus inside the ovary. The fertilized flower later yields fruit and seeds.

To have a balanced diet, you must have a correct amount of each food group and nutrients. A healthy diet is essential for good health and nutrition. It protects you against many chronic noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes and cancer. When you don't have enough of a vitamin there are certain deficiencies which occur.


Lacking Vitamin Deficiency disease Symptoms
Iron Anaemia Fatigue, weakness, pale skin, chest pain, shortness of breath etc.
D Rickets Fatigue, bone pain, muscle weakness, cramps, mood changes such as depression
C Scurvy Swollen gums, loose teeth, skin haemorrhages, easily bruised skin, swollen legs etc.
Calcium Hypocalcemia Confusion, numbness, depression, hallucinations, easy fracturing of bones.
